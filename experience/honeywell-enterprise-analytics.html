Honeywell · Backend Engineer · Jul 2022 to Nov 2024

Enterprise Analytics

Snowflake · SAP · SFDC · Azure · Fuzzy matching

I delivered analytics that tied Snowflake, SAP, Salesforce, and Azure usage data to operational outcomes: maintenance dashboards by asset and site surfaced cost drivers, and a utilization-based cloud cost monitor helped rein in waste. I also shipped fuzzy entity matching that materially improved how customer records lined up across systems.

For maintenance and reliability stakeholders, I built dashboards that slice spend and downtime by machine, line, and site, making it obvious which assets were driving the largest share of work orders and spare-part cost. That visibility supported a roughly 15% reduction in avoidable maintenance expense by prioritizing the right interventions.

Separately, I engineered a cloud cost monitoring platform that joins utilization signals from Azure and related sources with contract and allocation metadata. The goal was to catch over-provisioning and orphaned resources before they appear on the invoice; in practice the organization reduced relevant cloud spend by about 20% in the tracked environments.

Customer and asset identity is fragmented across SAP, Salesforce, and custom databases. I implemented a fuzzy matching pipeline for entity resolution (tuned thresholds, blocking keys, and human-review queues for edge cases) that lifted mapping accuracy across key datasets by approximately 35%, which downstream reporting and integrations depended on.
